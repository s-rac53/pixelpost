    We at PPP call ourselves pixelheads.

    Our reply is inspired by a great deal of fortitude as, as everchanging as the VFX business gets, the only non-impetuous virtue it bears is the perpectual creativity alongside technology and we endeavour to cushion and study the very pace of it. PPP is driven to study every intricacies and nuances of the magnum changes in the VFX realm and to survive art in its humble form.
